Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my monthly subscription anytime?

Yes, subscriptions are billed month-to-month and can be canceled at any time from your billing dashboard. There are no long-term contracts for the Pro plan.

How are delivery fees calculated?

Delivery fees are calculated based on the distance between the equipment yard and your job site, as well as the weight and dimensions of the machinery being transported.

Are there discounts for long-term rentals?

Absolutely. For rentals longer than 30 days, we automatically apply a weekly discount. For Enterprise clients, custom rates are negotiated for multi-month projects.

Need a custom quote?

For large-scale construction projects or specialized fleet requirements, our sales team is here to help.
